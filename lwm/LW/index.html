Who We Are

Cork's community
leisure network

LeisureWorld Cork is a community focused leisure network, operated by LW Management on behalf of Cork City Council.

Our mission is simple: make health, fitness, and recreation accessible to everyone.

Since opening our first centre in 1997, we've grown across Cork, bringing high-quality facilities into local communities with inclusive pricing and a genuinely welcoming atmosphere.

At the heart of everything we do is our ethos: Every Body Belongs.

01

Rooted in Cork

Community-owned and locally run for over 25 years. We reinvest in our facilities and programmes — not shareholders.

02

Accessible pricing

Flexible memberships, family rates, and concession passes — so cost is never a barrier to staying active.

03

Qualified expertise

Accredited, experienced staff genuinely invested in your progress — from swimming lessons to personal training.

04

Everyone welcome

All ages, all abilities. Fully accessible centres with adapted sessions and inclusive programmes for every stage of life.
